Wildlife Tips

TIP: KEEP CATS INDOORS

Wandering cats can be detrimental to native wildlife. Keep your furry friend inside to protect your feathered ones.

TIP: DON'T KIDNAP BABY BIRDS

Fledgling birds often don't need rescuing. Even if they cannot fly, their parents will usually be there to look after them in this time

TIP: HUMAN FOOD IS BAD FOR BIRDS

Although they will regularly beg for your snacks, feeding native birds inappropriate food, such as bread and sandwich meat, can make them very sick.

TIP: ONLY USE FINE BIRD NETTING

Bird nettings with large gaps can easily ensnare birds or flying foxes. Nettings should have holes no larger than your pinky finger, and should be stretched away from branches.
